Information

The Buenavista eRehistro is designed to handle the civil registry needs of residents of Buenavista, as well as Filipinos living outside the municipality whose Birth, Marriage, or Death Certificates are officially registered in Buenavista.

Did you know that a Birth Certificate serves as a “gateway” to countless opportunities? It is a vital document required when transacting with both government and private institutions such as the Department of Education (DepEd), Department of Foreign Affairs (DFA), Land Transportation Office (LTO), PhilHealth, GSIS, SSS, COMELEC, insurance providers, and many others.

Announcement

The Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) and the Local Government of Buenavista have entered into a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) to provide 1,582 free late birth registrations for qualified Buenavista residents—particularly members of Indigenous Peoples (IP), Muslim Filipinos, and families from the most disadvantaged sectors—through the Birth Registration Assistance Project (BRAP).
